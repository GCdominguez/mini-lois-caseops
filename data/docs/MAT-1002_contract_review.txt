Matter: Acme Supply Contract Review
Client: Acme Supply LLC
Type: Commercial Contract

Client request:
Review vendor master services agreement before renewal. Client wants to reduce operational risk and avoid automatic renewal surprises.

Important clauses:
Section 4.2 allows the vendor to increase fees by up to 12% annually with 30 days notice. Section 6.1 limits vendor liability to fees paid in the prior three months. Section 8.3 includes automatic renewal for one-year terms unless either party gives written notice 60 days before the renewal date.

Client concerns:
Acme is uncomfortable with the liability cap and wants a cure period for service failures. They also want a clear data return/deletion obligation at termination.

Recommended redlines:
- Cap annual fee increases at 5% or CPI, whichever is lower.
- Increase liability cap to 12 months of fees for general claims.
- Exclude confidentiality, data security, and indemnity from the liability cap.
- Add 30-day cure period for repeated service-level failures.
- Add termination assistance and data export language.

Deadline:
Client wants comments by June 30, 2026.
